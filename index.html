FAQs and Support

More than a logbook.
A road safety tool.

The Graduated Driver Licensing program exists to create safer drivers. Our app was built with that mission at its core, not as an afterthought.

📖
Fully Automatic Trip Recording
The app automatically captures start and end time, start and end location, weather conditions, and whether the drive took place during the day or night, so learners can focus on driving, not logging.
🎯
Smart Practice Suggestions
Analyses each learner's trip history to identify gaps in driving (e.g., night hours, wet conditions, and unsealed roads) then prompts learners to practice underdeveloped skills before their test.
📊
Driving Summary & Insights
A personalised breakdown of driving conditions, road types, and times, giving learners and supervisors a clear picture of experience breadth.
🎓
Structured Learning Goals
Progressive learning levels guide learners from foundational skills through to advanced experience, tied to real driving competencies with text, images and video supported.
📢
Government Messaging Campaigns
Deliver targeted in-app messages based on location, milestone, or campaign, directly from your department at the moments that matter most.

Over a decade of research
built into every feature.

Roundtrip began as a PhD research project at Queensland University of Technology, exploring how gamification and behavioural design can promote safer driving in young people.

Since then we have published peer-reviewed research with internationally recognised leaders in driver safety, on driving behaviour, logbook effectiveness, and how digital tools can shape safer outcomes and not just track compliance.

Research insights directly inform app features, from structured driving summaries to smart practice suggestions that identify gaps in a learner's experience, built into every logbook.
